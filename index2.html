Mendelian
Randomization

Observational studies are fraught with issues like confounders and reverse causality. Mendelian randomization is a kind of instrumental variable analysis that uses genetic variants as instruments to infer causality. The analysis is performed by plinkv1.90 in bash and MendelianRandomization R package.
